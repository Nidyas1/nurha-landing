Our Story

Built on one
philosophy.

Since 1996, Nurha Business Group has operated on a single guiding principle — "everything we do, however small or big, will be at the best quality." Founded by Nuredin Hassen in Addis Ababa, what began as a single venture has grown into a diversified conglomerate spanning agriculture, real estate, manufacturing, and industrial services.

🌱

Rooted in Ethiopia

Every company we build creates jobs, drives growth, and serves Ethiopian communities — from strawberry fields to urban high-rises.

🤝

16+ Year Global Partnerships

Our Humana Milk partnership spans over 16 years, distributing world-class infant nutrition across Ethiopia.

🏆

Award-Winning Excellence

Four awards recognising our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community impact across our business sectors.
