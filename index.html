Our Story

One philosophy.
28 years strong.

Everything we do, however small or big, will be at the best quality. This single belief — held by founder Nuredin Hassen since 1996 — has been the engine behind everything Nurha Business Group has become.

Nuredin Hassen
Founder & Chairman, Nurha Business Group
01 — ORIGIN

Started from the ground up

What began as a single agricultural venture in Addis Ababa has grown over 28 years into a diversified conglomerate with nine independent companies, over 300 employees, and operations spanning farming, real estate, manufacturing, and mining.

02 — GROWTH

Expanding across sectors

From Raha Milk's dairy products to Nurha Marble & Granite's premium stone, each new company was built with the same commitment: if Nurha puts its name on it, it has to be the best. The group's portfolio now spans five distinct sectors across Ethiopia's fastest-growing industries.

03 — TODAY

Building Ethiopia's future

Today, Nurha Business Group stands as one of Ethiopia's most respected diversified conglomerates. From strawberry exports to Dubai and Djibouti, to high-rise real estate reshaping Addis Ababa's skyline — the group continues to grow, employing hundreds and contributing meaningfully to Ethiopia's economic development.

Our Journey

28 years of
relentless growth.

1996
The Foundation
Nuredin Hassen establishes the first Nurha venture in Addis Ababa with a single philosophy: uncompromising quality in everything.
2004
Agricultural Expansion
Launch of Addis Strawberry Farm — a pioneering operation that would eventually export to Dubai, Djibouti and Yemen, employing 110+ workers.
2008
Real Estate & Stone
Kenmusa Real Estate established with 90 million ETB capital. Nurha Marble & Granite launches, supplying premium natural stone to Ethiopia's booming construction sector.
2009
Right Fresh Farm
Right Fresh Farm established with 15 million ETB initial capital, expanding the group's agricultural footprint and food production capabilities.
2012
Global Partnership
Nurha becomes exclusive Ethiopian distributor for Humana infant nutrition — a partnership that has now spanned over 16 years, bringing world-class baby products to Ethiopian families.
2024
Nine Companies Strong
Nurha Business Group reaches nine companies, 300+ employees, and four awards — a testament to 28 years of unbroken commitment to excellence.
